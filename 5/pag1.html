HAUNTED MANSION

The front door creaked as you stepped inside the mansion. Cobwebs hung from the ceiling, and the smell of decay filled the air. A painting of a woman with hollow eyes stared at you from the wall.
Suddenly, a loud bang echoed upstairs. You saw a staircase leading up to the second floor and a narrow hallway that seemed to lead to the basement.

Do you go upstairs… or down into the darkness below?
