ONE SCARY NIGHT...

The clock struck midnight as the cold October wind whispered through the empty streets. You were walking home from a Halloween party, your costume still damp from the rain. Suddenly, the streetlights flickered… and went out.
From the darkness, two paths emerged before you:
— To your left, an old abandoned mansion, its windows glowing faintly from within.
— To your right, a dense forest, where strange whispers echoed through the trees.
Your heart pounded. The air felt heavy. Tonight, you would have to make a choice…

Which way will you go?
