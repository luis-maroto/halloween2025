The Dark Woods**

You run into the woods. The fog surrounds you like a cold blanket. The moon hides behind the clouds.

A deep growl shakes the trees. Something big is stalking you… You can hear it breathing… *right behind you*.

Suddenly, two paths appear:

→ A wooden bridge with broken planks that leads over a dark river.
→ A narrow trail covered in pumpkins that glow with an eerie light.

Your heartbeat is louder than your footsteps. You must decide quickly.
