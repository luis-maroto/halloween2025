ONE SCARY NIGHT...

Its Halloween night. You are walking home alone after a fantastic costume party. Your phone battery is dead, and the streetlights keep flickering… on… and off…

Suddenly, you hear footsteps behind you. When you turn around… no one is there.

You continue walking faster. But then… You reach a crossroads:
→ To your left, there is a huge abandoned mansion. The windows look like empty, staring eyes.
→ To your right, a foggy forest whispers with voices carried by the wind. Something is clearly following you. If you stay here, whatever it is… will catch you. Where will you go?
