Shattered Truth

Clara grabbed a chair and smashed it against the mirror. Shards exploded across the floor, and the whisper turned into a shriek.

The air grew still. The mirror was gone—but carved into the wall behind it were words written in something dark and sticky:

“You broke him free.”
From somewhere in the hall, a door creaked open. Steps began to approach.
