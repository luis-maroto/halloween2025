The Stairs Below

Clara ignored Room 313 and followed the faint tapping sound down to the basement. The air grew colder, heavy with dust and the smell of rusted metal.

At the bottom of the stairs stood a locked iron door. A sign above it read: “Do Not Enter.”

Behind it—whispers. Many voices. Crying, maybe praying.

The door handle felt warm, pulsing, as if alive.
